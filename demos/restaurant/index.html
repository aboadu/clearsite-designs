Our Story

From grandma's kitchen to yours.

The Copper Skillet started with a simple idea: serve the kind of food that brings people together. Our recipes have been in the family for over 60 years, and every dish is made from scratch with fresh, local ingredients.

Whether it's our famous fried catfish, smothered pork chops, or our legendary peach cobbler — every plate tells a story. We're proud to be a part of the Lawrenceville community, and we treat every guest like family.
