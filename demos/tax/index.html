About Us

Trusted by Gwinnett County since 2008.

COA Taxes, Inc. was established in 2008 with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable tax and accounting services to the people and businesses of Lawrenceville and the greater Gwinnett County area.

Our team combines practical experience with ongoing professional education to stay current with the ever-changing tax landscape. We take the time to understand your unique situation and deliver personalized solutions — not one-size-fits-all answers.

Whether you're filing a simple return, managing a growing business, or dealing with an IRS notice, we're here to help you navigate it with confidence.
